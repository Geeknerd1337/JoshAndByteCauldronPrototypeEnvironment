This is a function called when the weapon is fired. If the weapon can be fired, then the weapon will fire either a projectile or a ray bullet based on the firesProjectile property on weaponObject. In adition to this, it will play the relevant audio for the sound, also based on a property in the weapon object. This function also handles visual effects like the muzzle flash and camera shake.
